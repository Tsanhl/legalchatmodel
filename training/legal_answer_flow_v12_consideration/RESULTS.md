# V12 consideration corrective trial — not deployed

V12 continued from V11 for 20 conservative iterations at a 4e-7 learning rate
using a reviewed 1,010-word consideration-reform essay. The trial was intended
to internalise the distinction between *Williams v Roffey*, *Foakes v Beer* and
promissory estoppel after a live hallucination regression.

- Initial validation loss: 2.388
- Iteration 10 validation loss: 2.469
- Final validation loss: 2.478
- Deployment decision: rejected; V11 remains the deployed adapter

The corrective answer, runtime accuracy lock, full-citation repair and live
regression remain active. Keeping a worse validation checkpoint out of
production prevents a narrow correction from degrading broader legal answers.
